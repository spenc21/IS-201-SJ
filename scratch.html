Star Wars (my guilty pleasure)

Star Wars land at Disney Land California should be at the top of your list of places to visit as a Star Wars fan! There is a life size replica of the Millennium Falcon!

  1. Themed Dining Experiences
    • Oga's Cantina: This is the centerpiece for drinks, both alcoholic and non-alcoholic. Famous for its exotic beverages, you can try the iconic Blue Milk or Green Milk, the Fuzzy Tauntaun, and the Jedi Mind Trick, among others.
    • Docking Bay 7 Food and Cargo: This is the main dining location offering a variety of dishes inspired by different planets across the Star Wars universe. Menu items include the Smoked Kaadu Ribs, Fried Endorian Chicken Tip Yip, and Batuuan Beef Pot Roast.
    • Ronto Roasters: Known for its large pod-racing engine firing a barbecue spit, this eatery offers a delicious Ronto Wrap, made with roasted pork and grilled sausage, as well as other snack-type options.
    • Kat Sakas Kettle: For something on the sweeter side, you can try a variety of flavored popcorn mixes at this market stall.
  2. Character Encounters
    • Chewbacca: The beloved Wookiee can often be seen in the area around the Millennium Falcon or at the Resistance encampment.
    • Rey: This key figure from the sequel trilogy frequently appears near the Resistance supply post, interacting with visitors and leading operations against the First Order.
    • Stormtroopers: These iconic soldiers of the First Order patrol the area, interact with visitors, and add to the ambiance of a controlled sector.
    • R2-D2: The famous astromech droid can be spotted rolling around, beeping and whistling at the delighted fans.
  3. Interactive Attractions
    • Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run - This interactive ride puts you in the cockpit of the famous Millennium Falcon. You and your team are assigned roles (pilot, gunner, or engineer) to complete a smuggling mission. The success of the mission depends on your group's performance.
    • Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance - Considered one of the most advanced and immersive Disney rides, this attraction places you in the middle of a battle between the Resistance and the First Order. You'll be captured aboard a Star Destroyer, escape from Stormtroopers, and face off against Kylo Ren.
